Advanced Fertility & IVF Care

State-of-the-art laboratory technologies coupled with deeply empathetic, personalized clinical protocols.

Assisted Reproductive Technology

Empowering Your Journey

To Parenthood

At Oak Specialist Hospital, we combine world-class medical innovation with compassionate care to turn family dreams into reality. Our pioneering IVF specialist clinic features custom-tailored therapies to maximize success rates at every stage of your journey.

ART Services Available:

In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

The standard-bearer of fertility treatments, fertilizing mature eggs with sperm in our ultra-modern laboratory before safe embryo transfer.

Intra Uterine Insemination (IUI)

A gentle, lower-intervention technique focusing on placing washed and highly concentrated sperm directly inside the uterus around ovulation.

Ovulation Induction

Utilizing tailored hormone therapies to stimulate the natural development and release of high-quality eggs, optimizing timing for conception.

Egg & Sperm Freezing

Secure, premium cryopreservation programs designed to preserve eggs, sperm, or embryos, safeguarding future family options.

Ovarian Rejuvenation (PRP)

An innovative therapy utilizing cell-enriching Platelet-Rich Plasma to naturally stimulate and revitalize ovarian tissue and egg quality.

Thawing Services

Precision-controlled thawing procedures for cryopreserved embryos or gametes to ensure the highest viability and success during transfer cycles.
