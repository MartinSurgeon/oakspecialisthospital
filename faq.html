Clinical & Patient Care

Find quick answers about scheduling consultations, laboratory testing, and outpatient clinic services.

  • What are Oak Specialist Hospital's operating hours?

    Oak Specialist Hospital is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including weekends and public holidays. Our outpatient clinics, pharmacy, general laboratory, and emergency services are always fully operational to serve you.

  • Do I need a referral to book a consultation?

    No, referral letters are not mandatory. You can walk in or book an appointment directly online or via phone for any of our clinical specialists, including Gynaecology, Paediatrics, and IVF consultation.

  • How can I schedule an ultrasound or laboratory scan?

    You can schedule diagnostic tests directly by visiting our hospital or booking online. Our advanced diagnostics wing features high-resolution ultrasound imaging and a fully automated pathology laboratory with fast turnaround times.

IVF & Fertility Care

Learn about our state-of-the-art fertility clinic and advanced reproductive services in Kumasi.

  • What fertility treatments are available?

    We offer a comprehensive range of fertility solutions including In Vitro Fertilization (IVF), Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI), Intrauterine Insemination (IUI), egg and sperm donation programs, cryopreservation, and advanced embryology services.

  • What is the success rate of IVF at Oak?

    Our fertility center leverages state-of-the-art laboratory equipment and seasoned embryologists. While success rates depend heavily on maternal age and individual clinical history, our clinical outcomes consistently rank among the highest in the region.

  • Is patient information kept confidential?

    Absolutely. At Oak Specialist Hospital, strict patient confidentiality is our highest ethical priority. All fertility consultations, records, and treatment details are guarded under highly secure protocols.

Insurance & Billing Support

Understanding clinical charges, methods of payment, and our accepted healthcare insurance covers.

  • Which health insurance providers do you accept?

    We partner with major corporate and private health insurance providers in Ghana. Please contact our administrative desk or call us at +233 (0) 509760659 before your appointment to verify your specific insurance coverage.

  • What payment options are available for outpatient services?

    We accept multiple payment methods for patient convenience, including cash, major credit and debit cards, and local mobile money transfers (MoMo).

  • Can I get a detailed medical invoice for corporate reimbursement?

    Yes. Our billing department provides comprehensive, itemized medical receipts and official reports needed for corporate claims, employer reimbursement schemes, or private insurance audits.
