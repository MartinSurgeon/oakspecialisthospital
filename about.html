About Oak Specialist Hospital

First-Class Healthcare &
Advanced IVF Care

Oak Specialist Hospital is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility located in Fankyenebra, Kumasi. Grounded in a wealth of international experience, we deliver exceptional, patient-centred medical care built on strict doctor-patient confidentiality, integrity, equity, and the highest clinical standards.

Fully registered under the Health Facilities Regulatory Agency (HeFRA) and managed in accordance with the Ghana Medical and Dental Council.
